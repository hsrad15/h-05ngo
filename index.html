Are you aware how NGOs work?

NGOs, or Non-Governmental Organizations, are independent groups that work towards social, environmental, cultural, or humanitarian goals. They operate independently from the government and are usually non-profit. NGOs can work on a wide range of issues including human rights, environmental protection, health, education, and more. They rely on donations, grants, and volunteers to carry out their missions.
